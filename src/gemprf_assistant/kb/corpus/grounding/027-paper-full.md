V3 in the GEM-pRF paper. V3 is a visual area region of the human visual cortex. The paper introduction names V1, V2, VP, V3, and V4 together as visual area regions that Sereno et al. (1995) functionally segmented on the cortical surface using fMRI-based retinotopic mapping with periodic visual stimulation. The paper does not define V3's functional role beyond naming it as a visual area.
