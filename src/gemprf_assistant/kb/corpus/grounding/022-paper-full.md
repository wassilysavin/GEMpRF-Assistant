prfanalyze-vista in the GEM-pRF paper. prfanalyze-vista is the Docker container distribution of mrVista (version 2.3.1_3.1.2; Lerma-Usabiaga et al., 2020) used in the paper's empirical comparison. The paper computes mrVista pRF estimates with its default configuration through this container.
