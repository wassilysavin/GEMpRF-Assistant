T1w in GEM-pRF. T1w is one of four values the BIDS space field accepts (alongside fsnative, fsaverage, and all). T1w refers to the volumetric (T1-weighted) reference space; the BIDS handler filters derivatives to this volumetric space. Selecting all overrides the per-subject choice and loads every available space variant.
