V1 in the GEM-pRF paper. V1 is the primary visual cortex. The paper uses V1 as the region of interest for single-step fine fitting validation on the first 22 subjects of the NYU retinotopy dataset, as the ROI for the empirical comparison against mrVista, and as the cortical area shown in the eccentricity, polar angle, and pRF size retinotopic maps generated for representative subject wlsubj001.
