V4 in the GEM-pRF paper. V4 is a visual area region of the human visual cortex. The paper introduction names V1, V2, VP, V3, and V4 together as visual area regions that Sereno et al. (1995) functionally segmented on the cortical surface using fMRI-based retinotopic mapping with periodic visual stimulation. The paper cites V4 only in this context and does not define its functional role beyond naming it as a visual area.
