HCP (Human Connectome Project) in the GEM-pRF paper. The paper references the HCP dataset (Benson et al., 2018) only in its discussion of related work: Ribeiro et al. (2021) trained a geometric deep-learning model on HCP to predict retinotopy maps from anatomical brain segmentation alone, without requiring functional scans. GEM-pRF itself does not use HCP data.
