FreeSurfer in GEM-pRF. The paper names FreeSurfer as the tool fMRIPrep used for cortical surface reconstruction during anatomical preprocessing of the NYU dataset (Esteban et al., 2019). The BIDS space field uses FreeSurfer-derived spaces — fsnative (per-subject surface) and fsaverage (group-averaged surface) — as two of its four allowed values.
